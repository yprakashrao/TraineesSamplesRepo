About Facebook

Facebook is a social networking site that
makes it easy for you to connect and share
with family and friends online Originally
designed for college students,Facebook was
created in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg,while
he was enrolled at HarvardUniversity.By 2006,
anyone over the age of 13 with a valid email
address could join facebook.Today,Facebook is
the world's largest social network, with more
than 1 billion users worldwide.After all,there
are already a lot of ways to communicate online,
like through email, instant messaging,and so on.
What Facebook unique is the abilityto connect and
share with the people you care about at the same
time.For many, having a Facebook account is now
expected part of being online,much like having your
own email address.And because Facebook is so popular,
other websites have worked to integrate Facebook.his
means you can use a single Facebook account to sign
in to different services across the Web.
